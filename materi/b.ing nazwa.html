PAPER WASTE

According to KBBI, waste refers to leftover materials from production processes that have no value or are considered defective. Every human activity, whetever domestic or industrial, produce waste that can harm the environment depending on it's type, quantity, and how it is procesed. To address this isue, the Indonesian government and international instutision have created regulations have created regulation to control pollution caused by waste.One common type of waste found in daily life is paper waste.

Paper has been used for centuries and is made from wood fibers or other plants.When it is no longer useful, it becomes paper waste-an arganic waste containing cellulose, heni cellulose, and Irgnin. Although paper waste can decompose naturally in the soil, the process usually taker 3-6 months, depending on soil. Therefore, proper sorting is necessary before disposal.

To reduce waste, one effective method is recycling paper into useful product such as recycled paper (KDU). Recycled paper con turned into various crafts like photo frame, pencil holders, flower vases, and shopping bags. This process not only decreases th amount of waste and prevents evironmental damage but also adds economic value to the products created

Processing paper waste through recycling benefits both the environment and the community. It helps preserve nature, reduce pollution, and provide economic opportunities.In conclution, recycling paper waste is an essential effort to protect the environment while giving new value to discraded materrals.
